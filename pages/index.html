Welcome to my site

I am a first-year Master student at Visual Motor Research Lab, Centre for Intelligent Machines, McGill University.

My supervisor is Professor James Clark.

My research focuses Image Matting using deep learning methods.

Recent Projects

• RoBoss - ImplementAI Hackathon, workflow supervision using object and activity recognition. (Check demo and story here: Devpost-RoBoss).

• FlappyU - VR Game: Winner of McGill Hackathon 2018 (Devpost). FlappyU was published on Steam in April. Check our Store Page!

• BAH! - Reinforcement learning for social good: a Gym-like public transportation environment focusing on rescue bus sending and bus scheduling. Currently working on porting to Gym. (Github)

• Tensorflow Speech Classification Challenge on Kaggle: Solution using CNN.
